Punjabi Guide

All of these are just recommendations from the community, feel free to Contribute

Dictionaries

Patiala University

Best overall dictionary. Any word you are looking for you can find here with pronunciation.

DSAL Dictionary

Similar to Patiala University dictionary, but easier to find example sentences for words in English.

SGGS Dictionary

Gives definition for words from Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji.

Punjabi Pedia

Encyclopedia/Dictionary strictly in Gurmukhi. Best online Punjabi Encyclopedia

Online courses

ELearnPunjabi

Best free online punjabi course taught in English with quizzes, vocabulary and history.

A Start in Punjabi

Video lectures in English for complete beginners. Also available in text

Learn Gurmukhi

Basics of Sikhi

28 videos in English that is all you need to learn Gurmukhi. Goes through history, grammar etc.

Billy Grosse

Great for learning to read and write Gurmukhi as it has helpful visuals

5abi

Text based Gurmukhi lessons, not the best for beginners as there is no audio cues but has great notes

Discover Sikhism

Text and audio based notes on Gurmukhi alphabet and vowels with games and quizzes.

Punjabi notes

Columbia University

Notes and flashcards on every part of the language including vowels, grammar, etc.

50Languages

Hundreds of phrases you could use in everyday life with pronunciation

PCRC

Simple PDF that goes through basic Gurmukhi concepts. Good for simple look up.

Books

Punjabi Wikisource

A Huge Library of copyright free and Public domain works.

Shree Guru Nanak Dev Public Digital Library

Thousands of E-books, textbooks, magazines and more in 30+ languages for completely free

APNA Org

English, Gurmukhi and Shahmukhi books available for download for free

Sooherang Bookstore

Gurmukhi books that you can read online for free. Constantly adding more books with time

Sacha Sauda Gurmat Parchar Society

Paperback Gurmat books for free (pay as you wish). Only available in North America

Tools

PU Spell Checker

Spell checker by Punjab University. Corrects spelling and suggests correct spelling, accuracy can vary but usually good.

PU Grammar Checker

Grammar checker by Patiala University. Corrects grammar mistakes and gives explanation on why it is incorrect.

Narakeet TTS

Text to speech to read aloud Punjabi sentences. Limited use per day but more accurace than google translate

Verses of Punjab

Translation of Punjabi folk songs with lyrics in English and Gurmukhi.
